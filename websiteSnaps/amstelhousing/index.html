    Rotterdam Brussestraat

    Well maintained 2/3 bedroom apartment on the outskirts of the city center and within reach of a variety of shopping, dining and entertainment facilities. Meent, Pannekoekstraat, the weekly market on Binnenrotte, Markthal as well as the subway and train station Blaak are all within walking distance.
    The property is located on the 3rd (top) floor with storage in the basement and a balcony at the rear with an unobstructed view over a green area.

    Layout:
    3rd Floor: Entrance with wardrobe and cupboard. Light living room (5.75 x 3.35m) with laminate floor. Master bedroom (approx 3.15 x 2.70m), 2nd bedroom (approx 3.65 x 1.80m) and 3rd study / dressing room (ca. 3.65 x 1.30m). Modern kitchen with 4-burner gas stove, refrigerator, washing machine and door to the balcony on the west. Fully tiled modern shower with toilet.
